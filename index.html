How I work
01
Research before pixels
I map journeys, run usability tests, and validate assumptions with real users before any Figma frame opens. Decisions should be traceable to evidence.
02
Systems over screens
I build scalable component libraries and information architectures that let teams ship consistently — not one-off screens that break at edge cases.
03
Outcomes over deliverables
A design isn't done at handoff. I measure against the original metric, iterate post-launch, and treat QA as part of the design role — not someone else's job.
04
Design for AI readiness
I structure interfaces to accommodate intelligent features — confidence scores, explainability patterns, and human-in-the-loop controls — so AI capabilities plug in without redesign.
About

I'm a UI/UX and Product Designer with 5+ years of experience building scalable digital products across energy, fintech, and SaaS. I currently design internal platforms at RWE, where I've improved analyst efficiency and cut manual workflows by 20% through clearer information architecture and interaction design.
